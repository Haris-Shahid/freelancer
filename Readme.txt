Management of its business as \"Freelancer\". Bills, Tickets, HowTo\'s and much more. Github project: (https://github.com/namaless/freelancer \"Freelancer\")

== Installation ==

1. Upload \"freelancer\" directory to the \"/wp-content/plugins/\" directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the \"Plugins\" menu in WordPress.
3. Go to Freelancer Settings and configure with your data.
